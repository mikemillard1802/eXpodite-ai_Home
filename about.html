How We Work

Operating Model

A structured, repeatable engagement model built for speed without compromising governance.

Phase 1
Discovery & Case Study Intake — Client submits business challenge. Claw Orchestrator analyzes inputs, maps to industry patterns, routes to specialized research swarm.
Phase 2
Architecture Blueprint — Multi-agent crew designs optimal swarm configuration. Human-in-the-loop review at every critical decision point. Output: client-ready deployment blueprint.
Phase 3
NemoClaw Security Configuration — Sandbox parameters set. Privacy router configured. Trust tier assigned (T1–T4). HITL guardrails mapped to risk level. Zero-trust permissions enforced.
Phase 4
Pilot Deployment & Validation — Swarm deployed in governed sandbox. Case study validation run. Acceptance tests executed. Audit log reviewed with client.
Phase 5
Production Handoff & Training — Client team trained via AI Crew Orchestration Labs. Ongoing support through subscription tier. Certification pathway available.
